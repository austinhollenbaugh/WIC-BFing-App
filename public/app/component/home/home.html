Utah County WIC helps mothers with breastfeeding.


We want to help you with your breastfeeding goals.


The American Academy of Pediatrics recommends breastfeeding for a year or more to provide the benefits your baby needs.


To help you with this goal, Utah County WIC provides:

  • Breastfeeding help by phone
  • In person one-on-one breastfeeding help
  • Breastfeeding classes to pregnant mothers
  • Free hand breast pumps
  • Free use of electric pumps, to those who qualify
  • Other breastfeeding aids, as available
